PRIVACY POLICY

GRUDA ARMADA — GRUDGE STUDIO

1. Who We Are

Gruda Armada ("the Game") is developed and operated by Grudge Studio ("we", "us", "our"), created by Racalvin The Pirate King. The Game is hosted at starrts.grudge-studio.com and related subdomains of grudge-studio.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Game, we may collect:

  • Account identifiers: Discord user ID, username, and avatar when you authenticate via Discord OAuth2. Similarly for Google or GitHub if used.
  • Grudge ID: A unique account identifier we generate and associate with your login provider.
  • Puter ID: A guest session identifier created automatically for cloud storage features.
  • Wallet address: A server-side Crossmint custodial wallet created on your behalf for in-game digital assets (NFTs). You do not need to provide a personal wallet.
  • Game data: Commander builds, ship designs, campaign progress, match history, and in-game settings.
  • Technical data: Browser type, screen resolution, and session duration for debugging and performance optimization. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • Save and sync your game progress across devices
  • Create and manage server-side custodial wallets for digital assets
  • Display your username and avatar in-game (leaderboards, multiplayer)
  • Improve game performance, fix bugs, and balance gameplay

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Discord — for OAuth2 authentication (we receive only your public profile)
  • Crossmint — for creating and managing custodial wallets and minting digital assets on Solana
  • Puter — for guest cloud storage when no Grudge account is linked
  • Infrastructure providers — hosting (Cloudflare, VPS) for serving the game; they process requests but do not access game data

5. Data Storage & Security

Game data is stored in encrypted databases. Authentication tokens are stored in your browser's sessionStorage and are never persisted to disk. Server-side secrets are managed via environment variables and are not included in client bundles.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data by viewing your in-game profile and Commander panel
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Disconnect Discord (or other providers) at any time via your provider's app settings
  • Play as guest without creating an account — only local browser storage is used

7. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, reach us at:

  • Discord: discord.gg/grudgestudio
  • Website: grudge-studio.com
