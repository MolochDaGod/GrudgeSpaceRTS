What makes it different

Real Solar System Scale

Zoom from a planet's glowing atmosphere to a bird's-eye view of the entire solar system. Every decision matters whether you're micro-managing an ambush or orchestrating a sector-wide campaign.

🌍

Cinematic Scale + Intro

Every game opens with a 4-second zoom from deep space (full sector) down to your home world glowing in orbit. 0%–4000% zoom range with logarithmic scroll and proportional panning at every scale.

0% – 4000% Zoom · Scroll to Skip
⚗️

6 Planet Tech Trees

Volcanic, Oceanic, Crystalline, Gas Giant, Barren, Frozen — each planet type unlocks a unique 9-node tech tree with passive bonuses and Void Powers.

42 Research Nodes
👤

Commander Corps

Train commanders at owned planets. Spec matches the planet biome. Level 1–5 progression grants compounding combat bonuses. Hero ships require a commander to operate.

5 Specializations
⚡

Void Powers

Nova Cannon, Tidal Wave, Crystal Shard Nova, Mass Warp, Void Rift, Planet Crusher — castable from your planet panel after researching the right T3 tech nodes.

6 Void Powers
🧠

Adaptive AI (D1–D5)

Five difficulty levels with distinct archetypes: D1 is passive, D5 runs multi-front attacks, manages full tech trees, uses void powers, and adapts to your fleet composition.

5 Difficulty Levels
🗺️

Star Map [M] + Minimap

Press M for the full tactical overlay: named fleets, if/then orders, commander tracking, void powers. In-game minimap: LMB = jump camera, RMB = move selected units. Player ships shown as triangles, enemies as squares.

4 Tabs · LMB Camera · RMB Move
☄️

Orbiting Resource Nodes

Moons, asteroids, ice rocks, and crystal deposits orbit each planet in real-time. Deploy Mining Drones and Energy Skimmers to harvest them for a passive economic edge.

4 Node Types
🛡️

Planet Defense

Build Laser, Missile, and Railgun turrets on owned planets — they orbit the planet autonomously and fire on any enemy in range. Max 4 per planet.

3 Turret Types
⭐

Ship Rank & XP

Ships earn XP from kills and captures. Rank 1–5 each grants +5% to all stats compounding. A Rank-5 veteran is 25% stronger in every stat — protect your best ships.

5 Rank Levels

Ship doctrine

Four Combat Roles

All roles are integrated into existing ship types — no separate build paths. Mix roles strategically: a fleet with no Repair ships loses wars of attrition; a fleet with no Star Splitters can't crack enemy lines.

Repair
