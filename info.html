QUICK MATCH
Fast PvE and PvP RTS rounds. Choose your commander, set AI difficulty, and battle for space dominance in self-contained matches.
  • PvE vs AI (D1 Casual → D5 Nightmare)
  • PvP matchmaking (coming soon)

  • Elimination or Domination win conditions

CAMPAIGN
A never-ending persistent universe. Your commander, your star system, your empire. Conquer every planet to join the galactic PvP arena.
  • Randomly generated star system per player
  • cNFT commanders with unique stats on Solana
  • Permanent progression — planets, bases, fleets persist
  • Boss planets, events, missions, guilds
  • Open galaxy PvP after system conquest
  • Player trade marketplace for resources & blueprints
  • Commander permadeath on flagship loss

GROUND RTS
Deploy to captured planets. Build bases, harvest resources, train armies, and defend against raids. Your economy fuels your space fleet.
  • Command Center → tech tree → full base building
  • Advance Wars-style voxel units (infantry → carriers)
  • Resource economy: Credits, Minerals, Power
  • Turret defenses, walls, shield pylons
  • Planet income flows back to space fleet
  • 10 unique planet biomes with distinct terrain
IN DEVELOPMENT
🎯
GROUND COMBAT
Drop into any ground battle in first or third person. Souls-like melee, ranged gunplay, and magic — with ally AI at your side.
  • First-person and third-person camera modes
  • 6 character classes: Warrior, Berserker, Ranger, Mage, Rogue, Gunslinger
  • Combo system, parry, dodge, heavy attacks
  • Ally AI companions fight alongside you
  • Defend or attack any active ground battle
  • PvP ground combat on contested planets
